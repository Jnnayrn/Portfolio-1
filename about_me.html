About Me

Hello! I’m Janine Irene Bautista, a passionate photographer based in the vibrant city of Quezon City. With a keen eye for detail and a love for storytelling, I specialize in Events, portrait and lifestyle photography, capturing authentic moments that reflect the beauty of everyday life.

My Journey

My journey into photography began at a young age . What started as a fun hobby quickly transformed into a deep-seated passion. After studying photography at FEU Institute of Technology, I spent several years honing my skills through various projects and collaborations. Each experience has shaped my artistic vision and helped me develop a unique style that blends creativity with technical expertise.

My Approach

I believe that every photograph has the power to tell a story. My approach focuses on building genuine connections with my subjects, allowing their true personalities to shine through in every shot. Whether it’s capturing the joy of a family reunion or the intimate moments of a couple’s engagement, I strive to create images that evoke emotion and preserve memories for years to come.

What I Offer

In my portfolio, you will find a curated selection of my work, showcasing everything from candid portraits to beautifully styled lifestyle shoots. I offer personalized photography sessions tailored to your needs, including: Portrait Photography: Individual or group sessions that highlight your unique personality. Lifestyle Photography: Capturing real-life moments in natural settings. Event Photography: Documenting special occasions with an artistic touch.
